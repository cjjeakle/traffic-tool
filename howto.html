This tool tracks your average speed over 1-minute and a 5-minute intervals. Ideally you can use this information while in dense stop-and-go traffic to avoid having to come to a full stop, making for a smoother overall drive. You can avoid coming to a full stop by traveling at the average speed of all your stopping and starting.

You can also compare the 1 and 5 minute intervals, to see how your average speed is changing over time. This is more for your own information, as it's not particularly actionable.

This Reddit thread has some great videos and explanations regarding traffic. It gives some insight into how driving at a slower, more consistent speed can (counter-intuitively) make things better.
